Welcome to my page!

I am a third year PhD student at Imperial Colle Business School where I am very fortunate to be supervised by Wolfram Wiesemann. Our research group is named Models and Algorithms for Decision-Making under Uncertainty, and I am a member of the Computational Optimization Group as well as the Data Science Institute.

Beginning October 2022, I will undertake a 9-month placement at The Alan Turing Instite as a recipient of the PhD Placement Award.

My research interests are robust optimisation, machine learning, computational privacy, as well as their intersections. I am specifically interested in developing efficient algorithms to solve hard optimization problems arising in privacy, machine learning, and business. To this end, in my recent works, I have been working on designing optimal algorithms in differential privacy, developing efficient column-and-constraint algorithms for distributionally robust classification, as well as providing tight bounds on convex maximization problems with applications to machine learning. My work has been recognized by the 2021 INFORMS Computing Society Student Paper Award as well as Imperial College London at the President's 2022 Address.
